Bugsy's Haunted Trail

Where Your Nightmares Come True

Dare to face your darkest fears on Bugsy's Haunted Trail, the scariest Halloween attraction of the year! Winding deep through a forgotten forest, this terrifying trail is haunted by the spirits of Bugsy and his minions—creatures that lurk in the shadows, waiting to prey on your deepest fears.

Legend has it that Bugsy, a notorious gangster from the 1920s, met a gruesome end in these woods after betraying his partners in crime. His ghost, twisted by revenge, now haunts the trail, bringing with him an army of supernatural terrors. From the eerie whispers of the trees to sudden encounters with Bugsy's ghastly henchmen, every step is a plunge into the unknown.

Will you survive the night, or will you become Bugsy's next victim?

Hightlights

  • The Ghost of Bugsy: Face the vengeful spirit of Bugsy himself in an abandoned speakeasy hidden deep in the woods.
  • Twisted Carnival: Enter Bugsy’s carnival of horrors, where clowns with sinister grins and rigged games lead to terrifying surprises.
  • The Graveyard Escape: Try to find your way out of a haunted graveyard while evading Bugsy’s undead mobsters.
  • Spider’s Lair: Beware the giant spiders crawling through the darkness, ready to ensnare any who dare enter their webs.
  • Interactive Scares: Experience live actors and special effects that will make you question what’s real and what’s not.

Bugsy's Haunted Trail is not for the faint of heart! Gather your bravest friends and prepare for a night of fear and fun. Will you make it through the trail, or will Bugsy’s ghost claim another soul?
